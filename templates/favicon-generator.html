FAQs about Favicons

1. What is a favicon?

A favicon is a small icon that represents your website in browser tabs, bookmarks, and other areas.

2. Can I use any image for my favicon?

Yes, our tool supports all major image formats. For best results, use a square image.

3. How do I add the favicon to my website?

Once you've downloaded your favicon, upload it to your website and link it in your HTML using the <link rel="icon" href="favicon.ico"> tag.
