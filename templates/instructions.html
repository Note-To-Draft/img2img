Standard HTML Website

Add the following code to the <head> section of your HTML pages:

<link rel="icon" href="/path/to/favicon-32x32.png" sizes="32x32" />
<link rel="icon" href="/path/to/favicon-16x16.png" sizes="16x16" />
<link rel="apple-touch-icon" href="/path/to/apple-touch-icon.png" />
<link rel="manifest" href="/path/to/site.webmanifest" />

WordPress

Go to Appearance > Customize > Site Identity, and upload your favicon.

Squarespace

Go to Design > Logo & Title, and upload your favicon (also known as a site icon).

Wix

Go to Settings > Favicon, and upload your favicon.
